The Best Movies According to Marcos

My top 3 movies of all-time. (no specific order)

A Clockwork Orange

It is hard to watch but it tackles really solid themes like crime, class divide, and psychological practices.

Spiderman: Across the Spider-Verse

Amazing animation and one of the best sequels out there.

The Conjuring

Good horror movie, not a lot of jumpscares and gets the job done.
